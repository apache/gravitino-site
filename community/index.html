Welcome to Apache Gravitino

Apache Gravitino community discussions happen primarily in GitHub and the developer mailing list, and on the #gravitino channel on the ASF Slack workspace.

Contribute​

See contributions for more details on how to contribute to Gravitino.

Issues​

Issues are tracked in GitHub.

Good First Issues​

If you are not sure on what to work on, look at issue tagged with good first issue.

Slack​

We use the #gravitino channel in the ASF Slack Workspace. To be invited, ask on the dev list.

Mailing Lists​

Gravitino has several mailing lists:

Developers: dev@gravitino.apache.org -- used for community discussions. Subscribe

Commits: commits@gravitino.apache.org -- distributes commit notifications. Subscribe

Private: private@gravitino.apache.org -- A private list for the PPMC to discuss issues related to community, security issues and to nominate new committers.

Apache Gravitino is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Apache Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
